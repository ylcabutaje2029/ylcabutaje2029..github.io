What is Minecraft?


Minecraft is a 3D game made up of blocks and it has three

dimensions, with different game modes, and it's where players

can explore their own world, craft different tools or blocks, and

build anything they want.



Who made Minecraft?


Minecraft was originally created by Markus Persson aka

“Notch”. Notch initially created Minecraft as a personal

project, but due to its huge gain popularity Notch sold

it(Mojang) to Microsoft in 2014 for 2.5 billion dollars.



When was Minecraft released?


In May 2009, Notch released the very first version of Minecraft which

he called the “Cave Game”. It was a simple game where

players could place and break blocks in a 3D world.
